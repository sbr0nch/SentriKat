## About SentriKat

SentriKat is an enterprise-grade vulnerability management platform designed to:
- Automatically sync with CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities (KEV) catalog
- Filter and prioritize vulnerabilities based on your organization's software inventory
- Provide intelligent risk assessment and prioritization
- Generate compliance reports and track remediation progress

### Key Features

- **Intelligent Priority System**: Automatic risk classification (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
- **Smart Filtering**: Focus on what matters - recent vulnerabilities, active exploits
- **Product Inventory**: Track your software stack and get relevant alerts only
- **Automated Daily Sync**: Stay current with the latest threat intelligence
- **Export & Reporting**: Generate reports for management and compliance
- **Professional UI**: Clean, modern interface designed for security teams
