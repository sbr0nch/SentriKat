# SENTRIKAT - PRICING STRATEGY
## Revenue Model & Pricing Framework

---

## PRICING PHILOSOPHY

### Core Principles

1. **Simplicity**: Per-agent pricing, no complex tiers
2. **Accessibility**: Free tier for evaluation, affordable Pro
3. **Scalability**: Volume discounts, enterprise flexibility
4. **Value Alignment**: Pay for what you use (agents)
5. **No Surprises**: Transparent, predictable costs

## PRICING MODELS

### Option A: Flat Tier Pricing (Recommended)

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Annual Savings |
|------|---------|--------|----------------|
| Demo | Free | Free | - |
| Professional | $99 | $990 | $198 (17%) |
| Business | $499 | $4,990 | $998 (17%) |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Negotiated |

**Pros**: Simple, predictable, easy to communicate
**Cons**: Some customers may underutilize

### Option B: Per-Agent Pricing

| Agents | Per-Agent/Month | Example (100 agents) |
|--------|-----------------|----------------------|
| 1-10 | $10 | $100 |
| 11-50 | $8 | $390 |
| 51-200 | $6 | $780 |
| 201-500 | $4 | $1,200 |
| 500+ | Custom | Negotiated |

**Pros**: Value-aligned, scales with customer
**Cons**: Complex, less predictable revenue

### Option C: Hybrid (Recommended for Scale)

| Tier | Base Price | Included Agents | Additional Agents |
|------|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Professional | $99/mo | 10 | $7/agent/mo |
| Business | $499/mo | 50 | $5/agent/mo |
| Enterprise | Custom | 500+ | $3/agent/mo |

**Pros**: Predictable base, scales with usage
**Cons**: Slightly more complex

## BILLING & PAYMENT

### Payment Methods

- Credit Card (Stripe)
- ACH/Bank Transfer
- Invoice (Net 30, annual only)
- Wire Transfer (Enterprise)

### Billing Cycles

- Monthly: Credit card only
- Annual: All payment methods
- Multi-year: Invoice/wire only

### Overage Policy

- Soft limit: Warning at 90% of agents
- Grace period: 7 days to upgrade
- Hard limit: New agents blocked until upgrade
- No retroactive billing

### Cancellation Policy

- Monthly: Cancel anytime, effective next billing
- Annual: Pro-rated refund first 30 days
- Enterprise: Per contract terms
